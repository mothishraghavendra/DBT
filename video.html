About This Video

This NPCI educational video explains the Aadhaar seeding process, which is crucial for citizens to receive benefits under various government schemes via Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT). The video clarifies the difference between Aadhaar linking and seeding, and provides step-by-step guidance for the entire process.

What You'll Learn

  • Understanding the difference between Aadhaar linking and Aadhaar seeding
  • How Aadhaar seeding enables receipt of DBT subsidies through NPCI system
  • Step-by-step process to initiate Aadhaar seeding at bank branches or online
  • How to check your Aadhaar seeding status on NPCI and UIDAI portals
  • What to do if seeding requests are unsuccessful
  • How to switch seeding between different banks

Who Should Watch

This video is essential for Indian citizens who want to ensure they can receive government benefits and subsidies directly through DBT by properly completing the Aadhaar seeding process.

Key Learning Points

  • Aadhaar Seeding vs Linking: Linking connects Aadhaar for ID proof; seeding maps it to NPCI system for DBT
  • Seeding Limit: Only one savings account can be seeded with your Aadhaar at a time
  • Initiation Methods: Visit bank branch, submit consent form, or use online banking
  • KYC Requirement: Bank verification and NPCI mapper update necessary
  • Status Checking: Available on NPCI website and UIDAI portal with OTP verification
  • Bank Switching: Mention previous bank details in new consent form

Important Portals

UIDAI Bank Seeding Status: myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in
