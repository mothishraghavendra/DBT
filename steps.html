Troubleshooting Guide

Steps to Enable Your DBT Bank Account

Follow these comprehensive steps to activate Direct Benefit Transfer on your bank account and resolve any linking issues with your Aadhaar.

1

Verify Aadhaar Bank Linking

Ensure your bank account is linked to your Aadhaar. This is the first and most crucial step for DBT activation. Visit the UIDAI portal to check and link your account if needed.

Visit UIDAI Portal
2

Contact Your Bank

Visit your bank branch or contact customer support to confirm Aadhaar linking and request DBT activation. Bring your Aadhaar card and bank passbook for verification. The bank will submit your details to the NPCI system for seeding.

3

Update DBT Beneficiary Status

After linking and bank verification, check your status on the DBT Bharat portal under "Know Your Beneficiary/Bank Account Status". It may take 24-48 hours for the system to update after bank submission.

4

Rectify Issues if Any

If your account is still inactive after following the above steps, contact DBT support or visit your bank to resolve any discrepancies. Common issues include mismatched details, pending KYC verification, or incorrect account type.

Important Tips

  • Only savings accounts can be seeded with Aadhaar for DBT
  • One account per Aadhaar: Only one bank account can be active for DBT at a time
  • Processing time: Bank seeding typically takes 24-48 hours to reflect in the system
  • Mobile number: Ensure your registered mobile number is active for OTP verification
  • Document requirement: Keep your Aadhaar card and bank passbook handy when visiting the bank

Next Steps

Once you've completed the above steps, you can verify your DBT status or return to the dashboard to explore more resources.
