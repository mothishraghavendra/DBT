Steps to Enable Your DBT Bank Account

Step 1: Verify Aadhaar Bank Linking

Ensure your bank account is linked to your Aadhaar. If not, visit the UIDAI portal to link your account:

https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in/

Step 2: Contact Your Bank

Visit your bank branch or contact customer support to confirm Aadhaar linking and DBT activation.

Step 3: Update DBT Beneficiary Status

After linking, check your status on the DBT Bharat portal under “Know Your Beneficiary/Bank Account Status”.

Step 4: Rectify Issues if Any

If your account is still inactive, contact DBT support or your bank to resolve discrepancies and enable DBT benefits.
