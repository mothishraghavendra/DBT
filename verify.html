How to Verify Your DBT Bank Details

Step 1: Check Bank Seeding Status via UIDAI

Confirm if your bank account is linked to your Aadhaar for DBT.

Visit the UIDAI Bank Seeding page:

https://myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in/

Step 2: Open the Official DBT Portal

Access the official DBT Bharat portal to check beneficiary status.

Step 3: Navigate to Beneficiary Details

Select “Know Your Beneficiary/Bank Account Status” from the main menu.

Step 4: Enter Your Aadhaar Number

Input your 12-digit Aadhaar number in the field provided and click Submit.

Step 5: View Your DBT Status

The portal will display the bank account linked to your Aadhaar and indicate whether it is active for receiving government benefits.

Step 6: Save or Screenshot

For future reference, take a screenshot or download the status of your DBT-enabled account.
