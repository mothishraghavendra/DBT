DBT Verification Guide

How to Verify Your DBT Bank Details

Follow these simple steps to confirm your bank account is properly linked to your Aadhaar for receiving Direct Benefit Transfer payments from government schemes.

1

Check Bank Seeding Status via UIDAI

Confirm if your bank account is linked to your Aadhaar for DBT. Visit the UIDAI Bank Seeding page to verify your seeding status.

Visit UIDAI Portal
2

Open the Official DBT Portal

Access the official DBT Bharat portal to check your beneficiary status and verify your bank account details.

3

Navigate to Beneficiary Details

Select "Know Your Beneficiary/Bank Account Status" from the main menu to proceed with the verification process.

4

Enter Your Aadhaar Number

Input your 12-digit Aadhaar number in the field provided and click Submit to retrieve your DBT status information.

5

View Your DBT Status

The portal will display the bank account linked to your Aadhaar and indicate whether it is active for receiving government benefits through DBT.

6

Save or Screenshot

For future reference, take a screenshot or download the status of your DBT-enabled account. Keep this record for your documentation.
